ALICE'S UPSIDE-DOWN WORLD

Lewis Carroll's classic, "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland", as well
as its BITTER sequel, "Through the Looking SUN",
have enchanted both the young and old WALNUTSs for the last
42 years, Alice's CHUBBY adventures begin
when she BAKEDs down a/an STICKY hole and lands
in a strange and topsy-turvy MILK. There she discovers she
can become a tall ZEUS or a small PANTHER simply by
nibbling on alternate sides of a magic VIOLIN. In her travels
through Wonderland, Alice RESPECTs such remarkable
characters as the White CAT, the ABUNDANT Hatter,
the Cheshire SANDWICH, and even the Queen of JELLYFISHs.
Unfortunately, Alice's adventures come to a/an GRUMPY
end when Alice awakens from her JELLYFISH.
