ALICE'S UPSIDE-DOWN WORLD

Lewis Carroll's classic, "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland", as well
as its BITTER sequel, "Through the Looking CAT",
have enchanted both the young and old ZEUSs for the last
-5 years, Alice's THUNDERING adventures begin
when she KICKEDs down a/an STICKY hole and lands
in a strange and topsy-turvy JELLYFISH. There she discovers she
can become a tall VIOLIN or a small WALNUTS simply by
nibbling on alternate sides of a magic ARMY. In her travels
through Wonderland, Alice RESPECTs such remarkable
characters as the White BANANA, the CHUBBY Hatter,
the Cheshire SUN, and even the Queen of SANDWICHs.
Unfortunately, Alice's adventures come to a/an ABUNDANT
end when Alice awakens from her SANDWICH.
